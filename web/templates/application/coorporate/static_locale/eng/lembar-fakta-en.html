Overview

AXIS is the fastest growing national GSM mobile operator in Indonesia. The company provides 2G, 3G and BlackBerry Services nationwide and covers the world through 418 international roaming partners in 165 countries.

AXIS aims to make mobile voice, SMS, data and content services available and affordable to all in Indonesia. The brand first launched its service in 2008 and introduced a different approach in the marketing of its products and service offerings by being simple, easy to understand and straightforward with its tariff plans. The company also challenges the market norm by providing honest offers without hidden terms and conditions.

Currently AXIS has more than 70% population coverage in Indonesia, covering Java, Bali, Lombok, North Sumatra, West Sumatra and Riau. AXIS HSPA+ Service is now available in major cities including Jabodetabek, Serang, Cilegon, Bandung, Semarang, Jogjakarta, Solo, Denpasar, Mataram, Kediri Madiun, Jember, Malang, Surabaya, Purwokerto, Tegal, Sukabumi, Tasikmalaya, Cirebon and will be continued to other cities in Indonesia.

Products and Services

AXIS’ pre-paid and post paid GSM services are the most affordable, reliable and transparent in the market and include:

  • Quality GSM Voice, SMS, MMS, and internet service
  • AXIS 'Internet for the People' consists of the following packages:
    • Best Saving Internet service (Pay per Use)
    • AXIS Pro
    • Internet Gaul
  • International direct dial, VOIP and Roaming services to 418 operators in 165 countries
  • 3G and HSPA+ enabled Internet connectivity for the home and on the move
  • "AXIS Worry-Free" BlackBerry Services – available in choices of packages: Basic, Basic+, Fun, Pro Unlimited, offered as flexible daily, weekly or monthly packages.
  • "Pinjam Pulsa" (airtime credit), first in Indonesian telecommunication market, allows AXIS customers to borrow prepaid credit Rp2000 from AXIS when they are running low on credit by dealing *911#. Rp500 service fee will be charged when customers returning their credited balance.
